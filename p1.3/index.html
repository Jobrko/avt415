1. Actor - Sinaht Valynore

2. Motivator - was invited by a DM friend to join his campaign

3. Intention/Intent - needs to get a fresh set of dice that go with her character and are relatively cheap

4. Action - browses the site that the DM recommended for starter sets. She ends up going through the specialty dice sets & normal sets before choosing a set that fit her the best. She also looked through the rolling trays to see if they matched the dice.

5. Resolution - She added the dice set she wanted & set a quantity, along with a dice tray that matched & placed her order online

Sinaht Valynore is a young woman who would love to start playing Dnd but doesn't know where to start. She asked a friend that she knows plays & he said that she should join his campaign he's going to be running. She says that she needs a new set of dice as her old ones have been lost & he recommends 'Goblins Horde' for starter sets. Sinaht browses through the site to find the perfect set of dice that represents the Changling character she has created & looks for a matching rolling tray. Once she has found them she places them in lher cart and places her order, eager to finally start playing Dnd!
